Introduction to Data Science

Data Science is the process of transforming that raw data into insights, predictions, and smarter decisions.

It combines statistics, programming, and domain expertise to uncover hidden patterns, build predictive models, and support evidence-based decision-making. From forecasting business sales to improving healthcare outcomes, Data Science shapes how we understand and interact with the world.

On this page, you’ll find a collection of projects where I applied Data Science techniques, including machine learning, optimization, time series forecasting, and data visualization. Each project demonstrates how data-driven thinking can solve real-world problems and drive impact.
