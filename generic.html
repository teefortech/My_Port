About

Welcome! I’m Tony Nwuzor

I’m a Data Scientist with a strong background in Operations Research, helping businesses and teams unlock the full potential of their data. My expertise lies in combining data-driven analytics with optimisation techniques to turn complex problems into clear strategies, smarter decisions, and measurable impact.

I work with tools like Python, R, SQL, and machine learning frameworks to design solutions that solve real challenges, from time series forecasting and predictive modelling to optimisation, dashboards, and data storytelling for stakeholders.

What sets me apart is not just the technical side, but also my ability to bridge the gap between advanced analytics and decision-making. I make insights practical and actionable, ensuring they drive real value for businesses and communities.

If you’re looking for a collaborator who can combine deep analytical expertise, optimisation skills, and clear communication, you’re in the right place. Explore my portfolio to see how I approach projects, and let’s connect to create something impactful together.
